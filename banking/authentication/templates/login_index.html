FAQ

Common questions

Here are some of the most common questions that we get.
What services does the banking website offer? The banking website offers a range of services including online banking, account management, fund transfers, bill payments, and loan applications.
How can I register for an account on the banking website? To register for an account, click on the 'Login/Register' link in the navigation bar and select the 'Register' option. Fill in the required information and follow the prompts to complete the registration process.
Is my personal information secure on the banking website? Yes, we prioritize the security of your personal information. Our website uses advanced encryption technology to protect your data and we have strict privacy policies in place.
Can I access the banking website from my mobile device? Yes, our banking website is mobile-friendly and can be accessed from any device with internet connectivity. We also have a dedicated mobile app for enhanced convenience.
What should I do if I forget my login credentials? If you forget your login credentials, click on the 'Login/Register' link in the navigation bar and select the 'Forgot Password' option. Follow the instructions to reset your password or retrieve your username.
